Motherhood Speaks

A 6-week virtual writing workshop and soft place to land for mommies who need to pause, write, release, and be seen in community with other mothers.

✦ Current Cohort — Summer 2026
Session 01
Gratitude for Who She Was
A simple thank you to her and how she got you here
We may miss who we were — but we can thank her for getting us here.
  • What do you miss most about who you were before parenthood?
  • What do you love about who you are in parenthood?
Awareness, Acceptance, Action
Sometimes we want to skip straight to action
The triple A's are a great barometer for where you are in the unpredictability of parenthood.
  • Think of a situation you are wrestling with. Where are you in the triple A's?
  • What do you need before you can get to the action step?
✦
Take it with you
Before you move to action this week, pause and ask: have I given myself awareness and acceptance first?
Session 02
Pleasure is Power
We deserve to feel good even when we don't feel good
Pleasure is not a reward for finishing your to-do list. It is medicine — and you deserve it now.
  • What brings you pleasure that has nothing to do with being a mother?
  • When did you last give yourself permission to feel good?
Sustainability Protocol
How to spot the cracks before they rupture
Like any well-oiled machine, we pay attention when the red light comes on.
  • What are the warning signs that you are overstimulated, overtired, or need a break?
  • If you had to create a safe word for when you are at your limit, what would it be?
🌺
Take it with you
Build your sustainability protocol before you need it. Name your warning signs now, so you can honor them later.
Session 03
Healing in Mothering
What does healing look like as a mother?
We are constantly healing in motherhood — it's time we celebrate that.
  • What is a win you've had recently?
  • What's one thing you can do for yourself to celebrate your win?
There's a Younger You Who is Screaming
How do we honor the little girl in us?
Sometimes the difficult feelings are a younger you asking for the mic.
  • Is there a younger you who wants the floor? Give her a moment. What does she need?
  • How can you honor the younger you this weekend?
💛
Take it with you
Healing isn't linear and it doesn't require permission. You are allowed to celebrate even while you're still in it.
Cohort 1 — 2025
Session 01 · Grace & Self-Compassion
"You Should Give Yourself Grace!" — Yeah, but what does it actually mean?
We explored what grace truly means, how to find it, and how to create access points to it in daily life.
✍🏾
Take it with you
Where in your daily life can you create one small access point to grace this week?
Session 02 · Joy & Humor
Humor in Pregnancy and Postpartum — Sometimes you just have to laugh
We acknowledged the humor that inevitably happens when caring for littles, and gave ourselves permission to find the funny in the hard.
🌺
Take it with you
Save one thing this week that makes you laugh — a video, a memory, a voice note. That's your rainy day kit.
Session 03 · Intuition Support
Trusting Our Gut Before We Google — Your gut knows.
We explored the balance between trusting our intuition and seeking reliable support — and how oppressive systems rely on us to ignore our gut.
🌿
Take it with you
Before you open a search tab this week, pause for 60 seconds. What does your gut already know?
Session 04 · Identity & Change
Loving/Accepting Where You Are Now — We are renewed in motherhood.
We explored how motherhood renews us, how to embrace the in-between space, and how to hold both the past self and the present self with tenderness.
🌺
Take it with you
The in-between is not a waiting room. It's where you live right now — and it deserves your presence.
Session 05 · Gratitude Practice
Before Anything, Say Thank You — We didn't make it here by ourselves.
We explored how gratitude seeds resilience — and how to find it and share it even in the hardest seasons of motherhood.
✦
Take it with you
Gratitude isn't about pretending things are easy. It's about finding what held you up even when they weren't.
Session 06 · Resilience Toolkit
When The Going Gets Tough — We always have a choice.
We built our personal resilience toolkits — tools for when we are overstimulated, frustrated, or overtired — so they're ready before we need them.
💛
Take it with you
Build your toolkit before you need it. Write your three reminders today and put them somewhere you'll find them when it matters.
