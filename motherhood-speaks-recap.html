Motherhood Speaks

A 6-week virtual writing workshop and soft place to land for mommies who need to pause, write, release, and be seen in community with other mothers.

Each session, we gather to write through it — the beautiful, the hard, the funny, and the holy mess of motherhood. These recaps hold the thread of what we explored together.

Session Recaps

What We Explored Together

01
Grace & Self-Compassion

"You Should Give Yourself Grace!"

Yeah, that's nice — but what does it actually mean?

Grace and where to find it
  • What does grace mean to you?
  • When was the last time you gave yourself grace?
  • What access point can you create for yourself so you can access that feeling?
Writing prompts we explored
  • Write about a moment when you needed grace and didn't give it to yourself
  • Describe what grace feels like in your body when you actually receive it
  • Write a letter to yourself from the mother you are becoming
✍🏾
Take it with you
Where in your daily life can you create one small access point to grace this week?
02
Joy & Humor

Humor in Pregnancy and Postpartum

Sometimes you just have to laugh...

Finding the funny in the hard
  • As much as we may feel overwhelmed and sad, we also get to acknowledge the humor that inevitably happens when caring for littles
  • What's a funny story you'll tell your kids when they're older about this season?
  • Text yourself the following to save for a rainy day:
    • A funny movie
    • Your favorite snack
    • A funny video from social media
Writing prompts we explored
  • Write the funniest thing that has happened to you as a mother that you couldn't laugh about in the moment
  • Write about a time your child made you laugh when you desperately needed it
  • What would your future self laugh about from this season of life?
🌺
Take it with you
Save one thing this week that makes you laugh — a video, a memory, a voice note to yourself. That's your rainy day kit.
