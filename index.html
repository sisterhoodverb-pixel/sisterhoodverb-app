Community Agreements
Before you join, please read and agree to our community agreements ✦
1
Safe Space / Safe Place

Vulnerability and honesty are honored here by not tearing down our sisters but building them up in every interaction.

2
This is a No Judgement Zone

Please refrain from judging someone's words, work, or offering an opinion without an invitation to do so.

3
Assume Best Intentions

If someone's actions or words gives you pause, then do just that — pause before responding or reporting.

4
0 Tolerance Policy

This is an affirming space. Bigotry, discrimination, or harm of any kind will not be tolerated.

5
What is Said Here Stays Here

This space is by us for us. Please protect it by not sharing what you see here without written permission.

What is this

A space built for real sisterhood

Sisterhood(verb) is a community-rooted creative practice offering workshops, programs, and spaces that use storytelling, arts integration, and facilitated dialogue to support wellness, belonging, and cultural connection.

This isn't another group chat. This is a practice space — where creativity is a tool for healing, community care replaces burnout, and your lived experience is honored.
