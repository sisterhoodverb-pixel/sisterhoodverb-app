Community Agreements
Before you join, please read and agree to our community agreements ✦
1
We show up with honesty and care

This is a space for real talk — not performance. We bring our full selves and hold space for others to do the same.

2
What's shared here, stays here

Confidentiality is sacred. Stories shared in this community are not yours to repeat outside of it.

3
We do not give unsolicited advice

We listen to understand, not to fix. Ask before offering advice or feedback.

4
We honor all identities and lived experiences

This is an affirming space. Bigotry, discrimination, or harm of any kind will not be tolerated.

5
We practice community care

We look out for each other. If something feels off, we speak up — with love, not judgment.
