👁 First Impression
Landing page loads correctly
Visit community.sisterhoodverb.com/app.html — you should see a dark hero with "Welcome home, sis" and a Join button. No jumbled text.
No login popup on first visit
When you first open the app, a login modal should NOT automatically pop up. You should see the page freely without any interruption.
Colors look correct
The hero section should have a dark (almost black) background with cream/terra text. Not light or washed out.
✍🏾 Signing Up
Community agreements appear before signup
Click Join — you should see the 5 community agreements and a checkbox. The button should be grayed out until you check the box.
Signup form works correctly
After agreeing, fill in your name, email, and password. The button should show "Creating your account..." while processing — not freeze or go blank.
Email confirmation screen appears
After signup you should see a screen that says "Check your inbox ✦" with your email address shown and clear next steps.
Confirmation email received
Check your inbox (and spam). You should receive a branded Sisterhood(verb) email. Check that the confirm link works.
Welcome email received
After confirming, check your inbox for a second email — a warm welcome from Tayllor with your first steps. Does it arrive? Does it look good?
🔑 Logging In
Login works and loads the app
After confirming your email, log in. The page should update — nav should show "My Account" and you should see the community feed with posts.
Onboarding modal appears on first login
On your very first login, a welcome modal should appear walking you through: welcome message, profile setup, joining a circle, and community agreements.
🌺 Core Features
Post to the community feed
Type something in the "What's on your heart today?" box and hit Share. Your post should appear at the top of the feed immediately.
Post preserves line breaks
Type a post with multiple lines (like a poem or list). After sharing, the line breaks should be preserved exactly as you typed them.
Like a post — count stays
Tap the 🫂 button on any post. The count should go up. Refresh the page — the like count should still be there and your like should still show.
Reply to a post
Tap 💬 Reply on any post. Type a reply and submit. It should appear below the post. The original poster should get a notification.
Join a Circle
Go to Circles in the bottom nav. Tap Join on a circle. It should say "Joined ✦". Go to your profile — your circle count should go up.
Post inside a Circle
Open a Circle you've joined. Type a post in the circle composer and share. It should appear in that circle's feed.
👤 Profile
Edit and save profile info
Go to Profile. Fill in your display name, bio, and location. Tap Save Changes. Refresh the page — your info should still be there.
Upload a profile photo
Tap the 📷 button on your avatar. Select a photo. The crop modal should open. Crop it and save. Your photo should appear on your profile.
💬 Overall Feel
Mobile experience feels smooth
Does the app feel comfortable on your phone? Easy to scroll, tap, and navigate? Nothing feels cramped or hard to reach?
The space feels welcoming
This is a vibe check. When you look at the feed, read the copy, and interact with the app — does it feel like a safe, warm, intentional space for women?
Would you invite another woman to join?
The most important question. If yes — great. If no or not sure — please tell us why in the notes. That hesitation is valuable.
