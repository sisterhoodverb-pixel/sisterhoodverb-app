Child Safety Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026 · Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Sisterhood(verb) is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. This policy outlines our standards against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and how we enforce them.

1. Platform Age Requirement

Sisterhood(verb) is intended exclusively for women 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly permit anyone under the age of 18 to create an account or participate in our community. If we discover that a minor has created an account, we will immediately suspend and delete that account.

2. Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Sisterhood(verb) has a strict zero-tolerance policy for any content or behavior that sexually exploits or abuses minors. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Content that sexualizes minors in any way
  • Grooming behaviors or attempts to exploit minors
  • Sharing, distributing, or linking to CSAE content
  • Any solicitation of minors for sexual purposes

3. Content Moderation

All content shared on Sisterhood(verb) is subject to our Community Agreements and moderation standards. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates this policy and to permanently ban any user who engages in CSAE-related behavior.

4. Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior that you believe violates this policy, please report it immediately by:

  • Emailing us at sisterhoodverb@gmail.com with the subject line "CSAE Report"
  • Using the report function within the app on any post or profile

We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly. We are also committed to reporting confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) as required by law.

5. Legal Compliance

Sisterhood(verb) complies with all applicable laws regarding child safety, including the PROTECT Our Children Act and other federal and state laws. We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations involving child exploitation.

6. Enforcement

Violations of this policy will result in:

  • Immediate removal of the offending content
  • Permanent suspension of the user's account
  • Reporting to appropriate law enforcement authorities
  • Reporting to NCMEC when required by law
