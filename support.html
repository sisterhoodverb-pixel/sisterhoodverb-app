Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Sisterhood(verb) app and tap "Join the Community." Enter your name, email, and password. You'll receive a verification email — tap the link inside to confirm your account and you're in.

How do I upgrade to a Full Membership?

Once logged in, tap the "Upgrade Now" button on the home screen. You can choose a Monthly ($8.99/month) or Annual ($79.99/year) membership. Payment is processed securely through the App Store.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  • Tap your name → Subscriptions
  • Find Sisterhood(verb) and tap Cancel Subscription

You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you've reinstalled the app or switched devices, tap "Upgrade Now" on the home screen, then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the subscription screen.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a link to reset your password.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by emailing us at sisterhoodverb@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account." We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days.

I didn't receive my verification email

Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's not there, email us at sisterhoodverb@gmail.com and we'll manually verify your account.
